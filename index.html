Bio

I am a Roboticist and Research Associate at NCART Lab, at Ryerson university. My work is focused on Autonomous robots, based on Lidar obstacle avoidence, fusion sensors, Navigation, Localization, and SLAM.

Born and raisein Tehran, Iran, captivated by technology development, sience and power of collaboration.

Nothing is more motivating than a Tennis game. Playing Tennis and Skiing are my favorite activity along with watercolour painting. Moved to Toronto after B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University. I worked at SDI Marketing as a Lead advisor for TMS location for 3 years. Meanwhile started my research on Smart Joint Methods at McMaster University. Thereafter, I joined Network-Centric Applied Research Team (NCART) as an research assistant under supervision of Prof. Alex Ferworn

My research interests includes Autonomous Robot Lidar Based or fusion Navigation, Localization, SLAM, Path planning, Obstacle avoidence Machine Learning, Computer vision, and hardware design. You can download my CV for my full profile.
